## Why static first

This site uses the App Router with `next-intl` so every locale gets real URLs (`/en/...`, `/ru/...`, `/ar/...`). That keeps SEO and sharing sane compared to client-only translation widgets.

## MDX for long-form notes

Blog posts live as `.mdx` files under `content/blog/`. The runtime compiles them with `next-mdx-remote` so you can evolve components later without a database.

## Contact form

The form posts to a Route Handler that validates with Zod and sends mail through Resend. A honeypot field quietly drops naive bots.
